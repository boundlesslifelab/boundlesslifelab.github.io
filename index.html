Habit Forming Workshop : Build Lasting Change

Transform Your Life One Habit at a Time

Do you find yourself stuck in the same patterns, promising to change but never quite following through? Are you tired of relying on motivation alone to build the life you want?

Our habit formation approach will help you break free from cognitive scripts, overcome the sequel mindset, and stop being a crowd pleaser when it comes to your own growth.

Whether you want to build a morning routine, establish healthier eating patterns, create consistent exercise habits, or develop professional skills, this workshop will give you the science-backed tools to make lasting change.

Join us for Habit Forming Workshop : Build Lasting Change, where you'll learn the psychology of habit formation and create a personalized system for sustainable transformation.

Workshop Philosophy

Before you embark on a habit, define your why.

Understanding your deeper motivation is the foundation of lasting change.

What You'll Learn

Why Habits Are Essential

  • Break free from cognitive scripts that keep you stuck
  • Overcome the "sequel mindset" of constantly restarting
  • Stop being a crowd pleaser and focus on your authentic goals
  • Understand the neuroscience behind habit formation

The Experiment Mindset

  • Approach habits as experiments, not failures
  • Learn from setbacks without giving up entirely
  • Adjust and refine your approach based on results

Workshop Benefits

Personalized Habit Strategy

  • Identify your unique habit formation style
  • Design habits that align with your lifestyle and values
  • Create implementation strategies that actually work

Sustainable Systems

  • Build habits that stick without relying on willpower
  • Learn to stack habits for maximum efficiency
  • Develop recovery strategies for when you slip up

Workshop Agenda

MODULE 1
Introduction & Purpose
  • Welcome and introductions
  • Understanding why habits are needed
  • Breaking cognitive, sequel, and crowd pleaser scripts
  • Group discussion on current habits and intentions
MODULE 2
Pillars of Habit Formation
  • The science of habit formation
  • Developing an experiment mindset
  • Understanding the habit loop: cue, routine, reward
  • Form discussion: sharing experiences and challenges
MODULE 3
Breaking Down Specific Habits
  • Analyzing different types of habits
  • Micro-habits vs. macro-habits
  • Habit stacking and environmental design
  • Don't skip the pain: embracing discomfort for growth
MODULE 4
Discussion & Engagement
  • Interactive habit design session
  • Peer accountability partnerships
  • Troubleshooting common obstacles
  • Creating your 30-day habit experiment plan

Who Should Attend?

This workshop is perfect for anyone who:

  • Struggles to maintain new habits beyond a few weeks
  • Wants to understand the psychology behind lasting change
  • Is tired of the start-stop cycle of habit attempts
  • Seeks a science-based approach to personal transformation
  • Wants to build specific habits like exercise, reading, or meditation
  • Is ready to commit to experimenting with new approaches
