Habit Formation Workshop: Build the Life You Want

Why Habits Matter More Than Motivation

We like to think we’re making choices all day long—but most of the time, we’re just following habits. These invisible patterns shape our health, happiness, relationships, and even identity.

This workshop will help you understand how habits work, why we get stuck in the same loops, and how to break free from unhelpful patterns—like people-pleasing, perfectionism, or procrastination.

Before you begin any new habit, define your 'why.' That’s where we start: with purpose, clarity, and community support.
