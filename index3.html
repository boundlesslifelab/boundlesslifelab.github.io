Workshop FAQ

Basics
Content
Format
Leaders
What's this workshop about?
Cutting noise, getting clear on what matters, and building systems to stick to it. No fluff. Just what works.
What will I walk away with?
  • One crystal-clear goal aligned with priorities
  • A broken-down action plan with daily/weekly steps
  • Strategies for when motivation fades
  • A practical review system to stay on track
Is this a motivational session?
Hell no. Motivation's temporary. This is about clarity, action, and systems you can rely on.
How long is the workshop?
Three parts, approximately 4 hours total with breaks.
What's the structure?
  • Part 1: Set the right goal - Align priorities across life
  • Part 2: Turn goals into actionable plans with milestones
  • Part 3: Build a system of check-ins that keeps you accountable
What methodologies do you use?
  • Warren Buffett's 5/25 Rule
  • Enhanced SMART+ goal-setting method
  • James Clear's Habit Loop and Stacking principles
  • Reverse engineering via milestone planning
What *isn't* this workshop about?
No vision boards, affirmations, quick-fix tricks, theory overload, or cringy "open your heart" stuff.
Will we apply what we learn during the workshop?
Absolutely. This is a doing workshop with practical exercises and immediate application.
Is this a one-time session or series?
One sharp, high-impact session. You'll leave with a system, not a sales pitch.
What if I already have goals?
Good. We'll refine them and ensure you have follow-through systems when motivation fades.
Do I have to share personal stuff?
Only if you want to. No forced sharing. Total choice.
How interactive is the workshop?
Very. You'll participate in goal mapping, habit-building exercises, accountability partnerships, and weekly review practice.
Do I need specific preparation?
Just bring an open mind and any goals you've been considering. We provide all materials.
Who are you, and why are you qualified?
We've lived this. We've built careers, businesses, and lives by setting sharp goals and creating systems. This is what we use ourselves.
Will I get post-workshop support?
The workshop gives you a complete, independent system. We'll provide resources to reference afterward.
How is this different from other workshops?
We focus on execution, not just inspiration. We prioritize practical applications and systems that work when willpower doesn't.
